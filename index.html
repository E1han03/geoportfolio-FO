About Me:

Currently working for Heading & Associates as an assistant surveyor completing re-establishment, feature and level surveys on the field reporting back to the senior surveyor and licensed surveyor at completion of job . Once the field work is complete, party leading surveyor will begin the office reduction using LisCAD and AutoCAD. My previous experience mainly involves Greenfield land development completing subdivisions and asset recording for developers and engineers. I have been working in the industry since 2019 however all up I have about 2 years of experience due to uni commitments as part of my work schedule. Throughout my journey I have had the privilege of experiencing both construction surveying and cadastral surveying using the two main brands of equipment Trimble and Leica.
